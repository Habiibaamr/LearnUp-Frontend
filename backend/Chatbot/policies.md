category: "Attendance"

## Policy Statement

Students are expected to attend scheduled lectures, labs, and tutorials. Attendance is recorded per session and may be used to determine eligibility for assessments.

Attendance requirements aim to support learning continuity, fair evaluation, and timely academic feedback.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all students enrolled in courses where the LMS is used for attendance management.

**Rule:** Attendance below the minimum threshold may result in ineligibility to sit the final exam unless an approved exception exists.

## Common Questions

**Q:** What is the minimum attendance required?

**A:** A common minimum requirement is 75% attendance per course. Exact thresholds may vary by faculty and course syllabus.

**Q:** What if I miss a lecture due to illness?

**A:** Submit an excused-absence request with supporting documentation according to the faculty procedure, ideally within the stated time window.

**Q:** Does arriving late count as absent?

**A:** Late arrival may be recorded as late and can count against attendance depending on course rules.

## Policy Statement

Course registration is managed through the academic portal during the announced registration window. Students must meet prerequisites and comply with credit-load limits.

Registration changes outside the official periods require approval according to university policy.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all students enrolled in courses where the LMS is used for registration & records management.

## Common Questions

**Q:** Can I add a course late?

**A:** Late registration is usually restricted and requires approval; use the add/drop period whenever possible.

**Q:** What if I do not meet prerequisites?

**A:** Enrollment may be blocked or removed unless an exception is approved by the department.

## Policy Statement

The LMS provides secure access to academic services such as attendance, materials, announcements, and assessments. Users must protect their credentials and follow acceptable use rules.

QR attendance is used to reduce manual errors and provide transparent attendance records.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all students enrolled in courses where the LMS is used for qr attendance & lms use management.

**Verification:** The system may validate lecture time, student enrollment, and duplicate scans; suspicious patterns can be flagged for review.

## Common Questions

**Q:** Can someone scan the QR for me?

**A:** No. Proxy attendance is misconduct; QR attendance is tied to account identity and may be audited.

**Q:** What if my phone battery dies?

**A:** Inform the instructor/support desk; alternative verification may be available under course rules.
