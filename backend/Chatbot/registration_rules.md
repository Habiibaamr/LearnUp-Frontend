# Spring 2025/2026 Registration Procedures

## 1. Registration Timeline
- **Window**: Sunday, February 8, 2026 to Sunday, February 15, 2026.

## 2. Mandatory Steps for Students
1. Students are fully responsible for their registration on the system.
2. The registration window opens from Feb 8 to Feb 15, 2026.
3. Access the electronic system after paying tuition fees.
4. Register for all course components: Lectures, Sections, and Labs.
5. Registration must be within allowed credit hour limits based on passed hours and CGPA.
6. Print the registration form and schedule and get them signed by the Academic Advisor.
7. If any issues arise or you need to complete your academic load, visit your Academic Advisor immediately.

## 3. Credit Hour Limits (For AI, Data Science, Software Engineering, and Cybersecurity)

### Max Hours Based on Passed Credits (for CGPA >= 1.9)
| Passed Hours | Max Allowed Hours |
|--------------|-------------------|
| < 34         | 17 Hours          |
| 34 - 52      | 19 Hours          |
| 53 - 72      | 20 Hours          |
| 73 - 108     | 18 Hours          |
| 109 - 126    | 18 Hours          |
| >= 127       | 17 Hours          |

### CGPA-Specific Rules
- **CGPA < 1.0**: Max 12 credit hours.
- **CGPA 1.0 to 1.89**: Max 15 credit hours.
- **CGPA >= 3.0**: Allowed to register up to 21 credit hours.

## 4. Graduation & Special Cases
- **Graduation Project**: Allowed if the student has passed **96+ hours** and has a **CGPA >= 2.0**.
- **Graduating Students**: Can register up to 21 hours if CGPA >= 2.0.
- **Summer Semester (Graduation)**: Max 12 hours if it is the final semester.
